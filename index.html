Features

This battlestation is full operational.

Modular

Bring your own data. Bring your own algorithms. Built for extreme configuration.

Lightweight

At 4mb (which includes a full stack of historical data), feel free to run as many instances as you want.

Expandable

Operations are diviided into simple helper programs to allow for further expansion and collaboration.

Free and transparent

Free to use and free to collaborate. No secrets or black boxes.
